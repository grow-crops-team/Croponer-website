Getting Started

Croponer is an AI-powered agricultural technology platform that helps farmers optimize their crop yield and resource management. Our platform uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze soil conditions, weather patterns, and crop health to provide tailored recommendations. We offer tools for disease detection, yield prediction, irrigation optimization, and more, all accessible through our mobile and web applications.

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Get Started" button in the top-right corner of our website. Fill in your details in the registration form, including your name, email address, and password. You'll receive a verification email - click the link to verify your account. Once verified, you can complete your profile with information about your farm to receive personalized recommendations.

You can start using Croponer with just a smartphone or computer with internet access. For basic features like crop identification and disease detection, you can simply take photos with your smartphone. For more advanced analytics, we offer compatible sensors and weather stations that are easy to install and connect to our platform. Our team can help you determine which equipment would be most beneficial for your specific farming needs.

Account & Billing

We offer several subscription tiers to accommodate farms of all sizes:

  • Basic Plan: Perfect for small farms, includes crop identification and basic weather forecasting.
  • Premium Plan: For medium-sized operations, adds disease detection, yield prediction, and irrigation recommendations.
  • Enterprise Plan: For large commercial farms, includes all features plus custom analytics and dedicated support.

We also offer a free trial for 14 days so you can experience the benefits before committing.

You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time by logging into your account, navigating to "Settings" > "Subscription", and selecting "Change Plan". If you upgrade, you'll have immediate access to additional features and will be charged the prorated difference. If you downgrade, your current plan will continue until the end of your billing cycle, then switch to the new plan.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and direct bank transfers for annual subscriptions. For Enterprise customers, we can also accommodate purchase orders and specialized payment arrangements. All payments are processed securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing connection issues with soil sensors, try these steps:

  1. Check that your sensors are properly powered and within signal range.
  2. Ensure your phone's Bluetooth and location services are enabled.
  3. Restart the sensor by removing and reinserting the batteries.
  4. Force close and reopen the Croponer app.
  5. Check for firmware updates for your sensors in the "Devices" section.

If problems persist, contact our technical support team with your sensor model and app version.

To reset your password, click on the "Login" button at the top of our website, then select "Forgot password?" link below the login form. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password. For security reasons, the reset link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact support.

For best results with crop identification and disease detection:

  • Take photos in good, natural lighting (avoid shadows or glare)
  • Capture clear, close-up images of the plant features or problem areas
  • Include multiple angles of the same plant when possible
  • Ensure the app has the latest update installed
  • Check your internet connection - identification requires cloud processing

If you're still having issues after trying these tips, use the "Report Problem" button in the app to send examples to our team for investigation.
