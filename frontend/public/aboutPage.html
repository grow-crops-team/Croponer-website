Our Crop Specializations

We've developed specialized expertise in these important crops to provide the most accurate recommendations.

Paddy (Rice)

Our system provides specific insights for paddy cultivation including water management, disease prevention, and optimal fertilization schedules.

  • Water level monitoring recommendations
  • Rice blast and bacterial blight detection
  • Custom fertilization schedules

Peanut

Specialized guidance for peanut cultivation including soil preparation, disease management, and harvest timing optimization.

  • Calcium and nutrient management
  • Early leaf spot and rust detection
  • Optimal digging time recommendations

Potato

Advanced potato cultivation guidance including disease prediction, storage recommendations, and quality optimization.

  • Late blight prediction and prevention
  • Soil moisture and irrigation guidance
  • Post-harvest storage optimization
